How it work

Requirement

Different startups at different stages, even in the same market are unique. Job requirement are influenced more by the startup's mission, goal and culture than the candidate's technical skills

We begin our process by listening, inorder to understand your requirements and expectations

Match and interviews

With a clear understanding of your expectations and our talent's career goal, we match you with the talent most passionate and aligned with your expectations.

To ensure you have a hassle-free interview process, we have routine internal culture-fit interviews to aid matching.

When we find your match, which usually happens in less than 48hours, we schedule an interview between you and the pool of talents.

Onboarding and trial

We trust our match process. However we give you four weekss, 30% discounted trial period. We anticipate our talent will require an initial one week training/onboarding with your team to understand your internal process and another three weeks of work. we believe the three weeks of hands-on testing is the final and ultimate way to validate that our talents meets your expectation

Commitment and continual bi-weekly review

Feedback aids development, promotes productivity and ensures that employees meet expectation. we provide three major feedbacks system

  • Biweekly 1:1 between managers in your team and the talents
  • Weekly retros between your team and our team review, our relationship, talent performance, amongst others
  • Internal retros with our talents working with your team
